#+TITLE: Solving Kryptos
Its well known that the Kryptos statue at CIA headquarters in Langley,
Virginia is not just a work of art, but also an unsolved cryptography
puzzle.  This is a write up of solving the first three parts, and
ideas on how to attack the fourth using the given hints.
