Features You'll Enjoy

The Deluxe 'Stere-O-Matic' Automatic Record Changer is the standard of dependability the world around. Your fine stereo records will retain their like-new sound quality almost indefinitely because this changer has a high-compliance cartridge with Diamond LP Needle and dynamically balanced, extra-long tone arm that offer as little as 3 grams needle pressure on the record.

Dual-Channel 60-Watt Amplifier and Pre-Amplifier provide 30 watts (peak) per channel to assure true high-fidelity tonal quality. The pre-amplifier not only has stereo input receptacles for radio tuners and other accessories, but also has stereo outputs from which a stereo recorder can be driven.

Stereo Speaker System includes two 12" woofers, for rich bass and mid-range tones, and two 3" tweeters for high tones. Tweeters are angle mounted to cover your listening room properly to obtain optimium stereo sound coverage.

Space for "Drop-In" Radio Tuner is provided. Directions for simple do-it-yourself connections are supplied with the tuner. The FM antenna is already built into the cabinet.

Support for Wireless Fidelity Streaming including support for Bluetooth and Apple Airplay. Plug in a USB drive with your digital stereo records and enjoy room filling sound from anywhere in your home.
